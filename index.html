Who I am

Web Developer based in London

Born and raised in the sunny south-eastern Italy, schooled between Bari (Puglia) and Florence (Tuscany), I inhaled sea, archaeology, history and… a lot of dust while digging as archaeologist! Although happy with my student and early work life, I moved to London to pursue a career in museums and the cultural sector internationally. One thing led to another and a museum internship turned into several different experiences in the sector where I developed an interest in Marketing and Sales in my latest roles. It was around that time that I started to think at ways to combine analytical with problem solving skills. I seized the “momentum” when I decided to quit my job at the time and pursue a career path as Web Developer.

Here I am, excited to start a coding journey freshly graduated from Le Wagon Bootcamp here in London! I am very grateful for the opportunities that this city has given to me (and many others) to reinvent myself constantly and never preclude something without trying it first. It has been an interesting journey up to now and the best is yet to come!
